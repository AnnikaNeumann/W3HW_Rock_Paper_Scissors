How to play - Live or die, make your choice

The rules of this game are pretty simple. There are two players and each of them has a choice of either rock, paper or *surprise* scissors.
According to the match between the player's choices, the winner is decided as follows - Look down.



  • Rock destroys the scissors to win the game.

  • Paper wraps the rock to win the game.

  • Scissors cuts the paper in pieces to win the game.

  • If both players make the same choice, the game result ends in a draw.
